# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 23, 2026
**Last updated:** April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Tesserak app collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We kept this short because the app itself collects very little.

If you have questions, email **pilzkoma@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. Who this covers

Tesserak is a single-player and social block-puzzle game for iPhone and iPad. This policy covers the Tesserak app and the backend services that support it (listed in Section 5).

Throughout this document, "**we**," "**us**," and "**our**" refer to the developer of Tesserak. "**You**" or "**your**" refers to anyone who uses the app.

## 2. What we collect

We collect only the data needed to run the game, save your progress, and show you on leaderboards. We never collect advertising identifiers, location data, contacts, photos, microphone input, health data, or analytics for tracking purposes.

| What | Where it comes from | Why we need it |
|---|---|---|
| Game Center player ID and alias | Apple's Game Center, if you're signed in | Verifies your identity and lets us show your name on leaderboards |
| Anonymous user ID | Generated on first launch if you're not signed into Game Center | Lets the app save your progress without an account |
| Username and friend code | Auto-generated or based on your Game Center alias | Displayed on leaderboards, used for friend requests |
| Avatar shape and color | Your selection from a fixed set | Visual identity on leaderboards |
| Game scores, streaks, and run history | Gameplay | Leaderboards, streak calendar, personal history |
| Daily challenge submissions (score, max streak, duration, seed, mode) | Gameplay | Daily leaderboards and completion history |
| Adventure level progress | Gameplay | Saved on-device only |
| Async duel records | Gameplay | Lets you play asynchronous matches against friends |
| Friendship list | When you add a friend via their code | Friends leaderboard and duel matchmaking |
| Moderation reports and blocks | When you report or block another user | To review reports and keep your experience clean |

We do **not** collect:

- Your real name, email address, phone number, or physical address
- Your location (coarse or precise)
- Your contacts, calendar, photos, or health data
- Microphone, camera, or biometric input
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Third-party analytics events

We do **not** use any of this data for tracking, as that term is defined in Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

## 3. How we use your data

We use the data above only to:

- Run the game (save your scores, track your streak, draw you on the grid).
- Show leaderboards and let friends see each other's scores.
- Accept and review moderation reports.
- Provide account deletion when you request it.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any form of behavioral tracking. We do not sell your data.

## 4. Who we share data with

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing, advertising, or profiling. The only parties that touch your data are:

- **Apple Game Center** — for sign-in and identity verification. Apple's own privacy policy applies to any interaction you have with Game Center.
- **Supabase** (our backend hosting provider, eu-central-1 region) — stores your profile, submissions, leaderboard entries, and friendships on our behalf. Supabase acts as a data processor under our instructions.

We may disclose data if required by law, to respond to a verified legal request, or to protect the safety of users or the public.

## 5. Data retention

- Your profile and gameplay data are kept for as long as your account exists.
- Deleted accounts are removed from our database within 24 hours. Cached copies may linger in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.
- Moderation reports you submit are retained for up to 12 months to help us investigate patterns of abuse.

## 6. Your choices and rights

You can:

- **Delete your account.** Open **Settings → Account → Delete Account** inside the app. This removes your profile, scores, streaks, daily submissions, friendships, and duels from our servers. This action cannot be undone.
- **Report or block another user.** Tap and hold on a leaderboard row and choose "Report or Block." Blocking hides that user from your Friends leaderboard and duel matchmaking. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
- **Stop using the app** at any time — simply delete it from your device. Data already on our servers can be removed by the Delete Account flow above before you uninstall.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (for example, under the GDPR in the European Economic Area or the CCPA in California) to access, correct, or export your data, and to object to certain processing. Email **pilzkoma@gmail.com** and we will honor valid requests within 30 days.

## 7. Children

Tesserak is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your region). If you believe a child has submitted personal information, email **pilzkoma@gmail.com** and we will delete the data promptly.

We do not serve advertising of any kind, and we do not use third-party analytics — which means the game is safe to use under the rules that apply to children's apps.

## 8. Security

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS. It's stored in Supabase's managed PostgreSQL database in the eu-central-1 region, protected by row-level security policies so that only you (and, for leaderboard data, users with the right to see it) can read your rows.

No system is perfectly secure. If you suspect a breach, contact **pilzkoma@gmail.com** immediately.

## 9. International users

Tesserak is available worldwide. If you use the app from outside the European Economic Area, your data is still stored in the EU (eu-central-1). By using the app you consent to this transfer.

## 10. Changes to this policy

If we materially change what we collect or how we use it, we'll update this page and change the "Last updated" date at the top. For larger changes, we'll also show a notice inside the app before the change takes effect.
